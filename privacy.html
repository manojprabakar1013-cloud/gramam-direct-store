Privacy Policy

How Gramam Direct collects, uses and protects customer information.

Effective date: 26 June 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, contact us or use our website, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, delivery address, city, PIN code, ordered products, order value and payment-related reference details.

2. How We Use Information

We use customer information to process and deliver orders, confirm payments, send order updates, provide customer support, prevent misuse and improve our website and services.

3. Payment Information

Online payments are processed through Razorpay. Gramam Direct does not store complete card numbers, CVV details, UPI PINs or online banking passwords. We may store payment IDs, order IDs and payment status for reconciliation and customer support.

4. Google Services

Order details may be processed through Google Apps Script and stored in Google Sheets for order management, reporting and customer support.

5. Browser Local Storage

Our website may use browser local storage to remember cart items, account details entered on the website, pending payments and order history on the same device.

6. Sharing of Information

We may share necessary information with payment processors, delivery partners, technology service providers and authorities when required for order fulfilment, fraud prevention, legal obligations or dispute handling.

We do not sell customer personal information.

7. Data Retention

We retain order, contact and payment reference information only for as long as reasonably necessary for fulfilment, accounting, customer support, dispute resolution and record keeping.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect customer information. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Your Choices

You may contact us to request correction or deletion of personal information, subject to information that must be retained for completed orders, accounting, fraud prevention or legal reasons.

10. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to services such as Razorpay, WhatsApp and Google. Their own privacy policies apply when you use those services.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for customers who can legally place an order. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children for independent purchases.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy when our services, technology or business practices change. The revised effective date will be displayed on this page.
