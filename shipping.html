Shipping Policy

Information about delivery areas, charges, timelines and order handling.

Effective date: 26 June 2026

1. Delivery Locations

Gramam Direct currently accepts orders for selected serviceable locations in India. Delivery availability depends on the customer's PIN code, product type and transport availability.

2. Order Processing

Orders are normally reviewed and processed after payment confirmation or Cash on Delivery confirmation.

Orders placed on Sundays, public holidays or outside support hours may be processed on the next working day.

3. Estimated Delivery Time

Estimated delivery is generally within 2 to 7 working days after order confirmation. Delivery time may vary depending on location, product availability and courier service.

Fresh farm products may require additional time for harvesting, quality checking and careful packing.

4. Shipping Charges

A shipping charge of ₹79 may apply to orders below ₹999.

Orders of ₹999 or above may qualify for free delivery, subject to serviceability and the conditions displayed during checkout.

5. Delivery Delays

Delivery may be delayed due to weather, transportation disruption, government restrictions, courier delays, high order volume, natural events or other circumstances beyond our reasonable control.

6. Correct Delivery Information

Customers must provide a complete and accurate name, phone number, address, city and PIN code.

Gramam Direct is not responsible for delays or failed delivery caused by incorrect or incomplete customer information.

7. Delivery Attempts

The delivery partner may contact the customer before or during delivery. Customers should keep their registered phone number available.

Additional delivery charges may apply when an order must be shipped again because the customer was unavailable or provided incorrect details.

8. Product Inspection

Customers should inspect the package at the time of delivery. Any visible damage, leakage, missing product or incorrect item should be reported as soon as possible with clear photographs or video.

9. Natural Product Variation

Fruits and vegetables are natural products. Their size, colour, shape, ripeness and exact weight may vary slightly from the website images and descriptions.

10. Order Tracking

Tracking details may be provided through email, phone or WhatsApp when available. Some local deliveries may not include online tracking.
