Cancellation and Refund Policy

Information about cancellations, damaged products, replacements and eligible refunds.

Effective date: 26 June 2026

1. Order Cancellation

Customers may request cancellation before the order has been packed or dispatched.

Once an order has been dispatched, cancellation may not be possible.

2. How to Request Cancellation

Contact Gramam Direct by email, phone or WhatsApp with your order ID and registered phone number.

Cancellation is confirmed only after approval from our support team.

3. Fresh and Perishable Products

Fruits, vegetables and other fresh products are perishable. Returns based only on personal taste, natural size, colour, shape or ripeness preference are generally not accepted.

4. Damaged, Missing or Incorrect Products

Contact us within 24 hours of delivery when:

  • The product is damaged or leaking.
  • An ordered product is missing.
  • An incorrect product was delivered.
  • The product is unusable at delivery.

Customers may be required to provide clear photographs or an unboxing video showing the product and package.

5. Replacement or Refund

After reviewing the request, Gramam Direct may offer a replacement, store credit, partial refund or full refund depending on the issue and product condition.

6. Non-Eligible Requests

A refund or replacement may not be provided when:

  • The complaint is submitted after the stated reporting period.
  • The product was stored or handled incorrectly after delivery.
  • The delivery failed because incorrect customer information was provided.
  • The variation is natural for fresh agricultural products.
  • Sufficient photographs or supporting details are not provided.

7. Online Payment Refunds

Approved refunds for online payments will normally be returned to the original payment method.

After initiation, the payment gateway or bank may take approximately 5 to 10 working days to reflect the refund in the customer's account.

8. Cash on Delivery Refunds

Approved Cash on Delivery refunds may be processed through bank transfer or UPI. The customer must provide correct refund details.

9. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are generally non-refundable unless the complete order was incorrect, damaged or could not be delivered due to an issue from Gramam Direct.

10. Order Cancellation by Gramam Direct

We may cancel an order due to product unavailability, incorrect pricing, delivery restrictions, suspected misuse or circumstances beyond our control.

Any eligible prepaid amount will be refunded to the original payment method.
